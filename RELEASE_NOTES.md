# BOXBOXBOX v1.14.0 Release Notes

## Season Break Mode

- New dedicated Season Break screen during off-season periods
- Toggle between Driver and Constructor championship standings
- Animated transitions between driver headshots and team cars
- Countdown timer to the next race (Australian GP 2025)

## Latest Tab Redesign

- Pinterest-style Bento grid layout with varied card sizes
- Quick access to Hotlap game from the feed
- Mixed content from news, videos, social posts, and podcasts
- Click any social post to jump directly to it in the Social tab

## Social Feed Improvements

- Fresher content on every refresh with improved sorting
- Better mix of accounts - less repetition from any single source
- New meme accounts added: dailyf1center, f1memesig, f1wow, f1_edit33, shaaaarl
- Reels open in full-screen mode when tapped

## General Enhancements

- News cards show more headline text
- Smoother Season Break hero animations
- Improved navigation between screens
